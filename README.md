# USD ⇄ NGN Remit & Profit Calculator

A web-based profit calculator for USD to NGN currency exchange and remittance transactions.

## Features

- **Dual Calculation Modes**:
  - Send USD → Get NGN: Calculate how much Naira a customer receives
  - Target NGN → Collect USD: Calculate how much USD is needed for a desired Naira amount

- **Real-time Profit Calculations**: 
  - Automatically calculates profit based on rate differences
  - Includes 50% fee share in profit calculations
  - Shows profit in both NGN and USD

- **Tiered Fee Structure**:
  - $0-$50: $5 fee
  - $51-$500: $10 fee
  - $501-$1000: $15 fee
  - Over $1000: $15 + 1.5% on amount over $1000

- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Clean Modern UI**: Dark theme with easy-to-read layout

## How to Use

1. **Set Your Rates**:
   - Enter the **Customer rate** (what you give to clients)
   - Enter the **Purchase rate** (what you buy NGN at)

2. **Calculate a Transaction**:
   - **Option A**: Enter USD amount the customer is sending
   - **Option B**: Enter the Naira amount the customer wants to receive

3. **View Results**: The calculator shows:
   - The corresponding amount in the other currency
   - Your estimated profit in both NGN and USD
